About us


Sheetal Bakers (India) Ltd (SB) was set up in 2015 by Mrs.Sheetal Joshi.It is situated at Samparshan park,Navi mumai(India). It was set up under the Colombo plan with the help of New Mumbai Bakers(NMB).Sheetal Bakery manages a selection of goods asked by its clients and works to maintain its supply in a recent and comprehensive state. Provision shops play an integral part in our lives. People turn to places like this to buy pastries, flour-based food: cakes, sweet desserts, loaves of breads and cookies.
